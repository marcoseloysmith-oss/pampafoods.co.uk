About Pampa Foods

Where Argentine quality meets British distribution.

Pampa Foods Ltd is a UK-registered food importer operating at the intersection of two worlds: the rich agricultural heritage of Argentina and the demanding standards of the British market.

We work directly with producers in Argentina's finest growing regions — the olive groves of Mendoza and San Juan, and the peanut fields of Córdoba — sourcing products that meet exacting quality standards and telling the story of their origin.

With our own freight forwarding operations in Argentina, we control the supply chain end-to-end. For our UK wholesale and distribution partners, this means reliability, competitive pricing, and a single point of contact from grove to warehouse.
